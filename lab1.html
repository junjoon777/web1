What is Liverpool FC?

Liverpool FC is a professional soccer club located in Liverpool, England. Liverpool FC is in the Premier League, which is the most competitive soccer league in England. It was founded in 1892 and Anfield is the home Stadium. Red Liverbird is the symbol of the team, becasue Liverbird is the symbol of the English city of Liverpool. Therefore, team color is also red. "You will never walk alone" by Gerry and the Peacemakers is used as a cheer song.

Why is it my favorite team?

Throughout my life, I was not really into watching sports until my junior year of highschool. During my junior year, I started watching Engish Premier League and National Football League with my friends. My younger brother and my friends were really into professional soccer leagues, so I was influenced by them. The most popular soccer league in the world is English Premier League and I had to choose a team to support. I was immediately attracted to Liverpool FC due to its playing style: fast and aggresive attack play rather than being defensive. I also liked the fact that Liverpool FC is strong against big clubs and weak against small clubs. During my Senior year, Liverpool miraculary went to the Final of European Champions League, which is the most prestigious tournament in the world. Although Liverpool didn't get the first place, it was enough for me to decide that I will support this team until I die.
